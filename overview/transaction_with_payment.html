End-to-end · Trade × Payment × Logistics

One purchase, three contracts — joined by linkedContractId.

A consumer in Surabaya buys a smartphone on Pasar Sapa from a Jakarta merchant. Payment goes through GoPay, fulfilment through JNE. Each sector — Trade, Payment, Logistics — runs its own Beckn message exchange under terms ratified by ION, and the three contracts stitch together through one shared identifier.

Consumer Andi Surabaya · 35-XXXX
BAP · all sectors Pasar Sapa Buyer app
BPP · Trade Toko Elektronik Jakarta · electronics
BPP · Payment GoPay QRIS · BI-FAST rail
BPP · Logistics JNE LOG-PARCEL · NEXT_DAY

Step by step

18 phases · 36 messages · three contracts joined by linkedContractId
1 Catalog

Three BPPs publish

Toko Elektronik publishes its product catalog with policy IRIs. GoPay publishes its payment methods and MDR schedule. JNE publishes rate logic and provincial coverage. Pasar Sapa keeps a fresh local index.

  • /catalog/publish ×3
  • /on_subscribe
2 Browse

Andi opens the app

Search runs against Pasar Sapa's local index — no live network call. The listing surfaces with its declared availability.status.

3 Trade · select

Quote the order

BAP submits item, qty, drop pincode. BPP returns a quote with structured breakup including PPN at the rate sourced from current PMK.

  • /select
  • /on_select
  • quote.breakup
4 Trade · init

Address + payment intent

BAP commits the consumer's full Surabaya address. Crucially, the BPP's /on_init returns a paymentRequest — amount, payee VPA, idempotency key — to be discharged via the Payment sector.

  • /init
  • /on_init
  • paymentRequest
5 Payment · select

Discover methods

BAP queries the Payment BPP with the amount and parties. GoPay returns supported methods (QRIS, VA, EWALLET) with the MDR for each — letting the BAP show price-by-method to the consumer.

  • /select
  • /on_select
  • methods
  • MDR
6 Payment · init

Get the instrument

Consumer picks QRIS. BAP /inits the chosen method; GoPay returns the QRIS string with an expiry and a paymentRef.

  • /init
  • /on_init
  • qrisString
  • expiresAt
7 Payment · confirm + status

Authorize, then PAID

BAP confirms with linkedContractId pointing at the Trade contract. Andi scans the QR in his GoPay app — the actual money rides BI-FAST off-network. GoPay then pushes /on_status [PAID] with settlementId and NTPN.

  • /confirm
  • /on_confirm
  • /on_status
  • NTPN
8 Trade · confirm

Bind the Trade contract

Now that payment is verifiable on-network, BAP confirms with the merchant and attaches paymentReference. Toko Elektronik (PKP) returns Faktur Pajak ref + fulfilment warehouse. Trade contract ACTIVE.

  • /confirm
  • /on_confirm
  • paymentReference
  • fakturPajakReference
9 Logistics · select

Bind a binding rate

BAP calls JNE with the actual shipment profile — pickup, drop, weight, declared value. JNE returns a binding rate with a quote validity window.

  • /select
  • /on_select
  • quoteValidityWindow
10 Logistics · init

Apply Framework Agreement

BAP attaches the active logistics-fwa reference (Pasar Sapa × JNE 2026 Q2). The FWA's commercial envelope supersedes per-offer policies.

  • /init
  • /on_init
  • fwaReference
11 Logistics · confirm

The third linked contract

Logistics confirm carries linkedContractId pointing at the Trade contract. JNE returns an AWB; the Logistics contract is ACTIVE.

  • /confirm
  • /on_confirm
  • linkedContractId
  • awbNumber
12 Status

DISPATCHED + PICKED_UP

BPP-Trade pushes DISPATCHED with the AWB. JNE collects from the merchant warehouse and pushes PICKED_UP — proxy phone numbers only.

  • /on_status
  • awbNumber
  • phoneProxy
13 Logistics · transit

Hub routing + live track

JNE moves the parcel through its Jakarta hub (P2H2P). BAP opens a /track session for live GPS, in addition to coarse status pushes.

  • /track
  • /on_track
  • IN_TRANSIT
  • OUT_FOR_DELIVERY
14 Delivered

Both contracts close

JNE pushes DELIVERED with a delivery-proof photo. BAP relays via /update; Trade contract → COMPLETE. Andi gets a push.

  • /on_status
  • deliveryProofUrl
  • contract → COMPLETE
15 Rating

Five dimensions, one act

Andi rates once. BAP splits into Trade ratings (PROVIDER, ITEM) and Logistics ratings (FULFILLMENT, PACKAGE_CONDITION, AGENT_BEHAVIOUR).

  • /rate
  • 5 dimensions
16 Trade · reconcile

Money, with proof

BAP previews then commits. The /on_reconcile carries PPN remittance, platform fee, and bukti potong references.

  • /reconcile
  • PPN · NTPN
17 Logistics · reconcile

Batched monthly

Per-shipment reconcile doesn't scale at marketplace volume — Logistics settles in monthly batches with isBatch=true, including FWA rebates and weight-dispute adjustments.

  • isBatch
  • FWA rebate
  • weight adj.
18 Payment · reconcile

PSP economics

Payment BPPs settle their own economics with the BAP — MDR, refund-rate adjustments, authorisation-success-rate reporting — keeping payment health observable network-side.

  • MDR
  • refund-rate
  • auth-rate
One identifier, three sectors. The Payment and Logistics contracts both attach linkedContractId pointing at the Trade contract. That single field is what lets the BAP — or any auditor — stitch TRADE/…/A1F2 PAY/…/G7H8 LOG/…/J7K9 into one coherent transaction story.
